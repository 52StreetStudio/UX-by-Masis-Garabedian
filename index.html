What I Do!

As a certified UX designer and researcher, I have performed monitored and unmonitored user testing sessions and came up with key findings and recommendations for several projects. In addition to creating a well written survey questions, I have conducted an A/B testing to see which version of the product works the best.

Analysis

  • Research competitor products and UI design trends
  • Conduct product owner and stakeholder interviews to determine business goals
  • Negotiate technical requirements with product owners, developers, and project managers to determine interaction pattern guidelines, extent of usability testing, and scope of interactive prototypes
  • Analyze quantitative site data from Adobe Analytics to determine conversion and acquisition metrics

Conceptualize

  • Partner with system analysts and product owners to articulate user stories, create low fidelity mock ups, wireframes, and interactive prototypes
  • Create customer journey maps with input from product owner and analysts to help pinpoint MVP product features
  • Contextual interviews with primary users in order to distill use patterns and environmental concerns

 

Design

CoolFigs App

CoolFigs app will assist collectors to be on top of their collecting game. The app will keep users up to date in what is going on in the collecting rom by providing users with the latest news from different sources, a database to track their collection, to order and preorder items directly from the app in addition to other cool features. Amazon Gift Finder

This research will focus on Amazon Gift Finder feature. Amazon Gift Finder is a feature provided by Amazon to help users filter and find the perfect gift that meets their boundaries from thousands of ideas and items on the market.
Part of this research will involve observing users while they find the gifts based on scenarios I have for them. Users need to use the filtering mechanism to filter thousands of items on Amazon based on factors provided in the scenario. (2018)
